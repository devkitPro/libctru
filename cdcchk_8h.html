enum CodecI2sLine

I2S line enumeration.

Enumerator
CODEC_I2S_LINE_1 

Primary I2S line, used by DSP/Mic (configurable)/GBA sound controller.

CODEC_I2S_LINE_2 

Secondary I2S line, used by CSND hardware.
